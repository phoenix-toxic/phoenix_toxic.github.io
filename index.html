Special High-quality Services

Chemical Peeling

Chemical Peeling has been used since time immemorial to smoothen and improve the skin. Chemical Peeling is an application of one or more chemical agents to the skin so as to cause controlled chemical burns leading to exfoliation and resurfacing.

Thread Lift

With age there occurs sagging of facial skin and muscles.Thread lift is a minimally invasive procedure for lifting the skin. Here threads are inserted under skin to produce a subtle but visible lift in the skin.

Dermal Fillers

Dermal fillers are soft gel like substances that are injected under the skin. They can address a number of concerns including correcting deep under eye circles, lifting of cheek bones,volumization of lips , smoothing of lip lines

Botox

Botox uses Botulinum Toxin type A. It is an injectable used to reduce the appearance of facial wrinkles. Botox is minimally invasive ,safe, effective treatment for fine lines and wrinkles.
